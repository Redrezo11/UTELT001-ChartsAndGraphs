Templates to describe Charts and Graphs

Line graph template:

 1.) This line graph is about __________.

2.) The Trend of this graph shows that ___________.

vocab words: amount, highest, lowest, maximum, minimum, volatile, stable, constant at.

(ex: Toyota cars are volatile in price from a maximum of 13k USD to a minimum of 5k USD)
(ex2: Toyota cars are stable in price from a maximum of 13k USD to a minimum of 12.5k USD) (ex3: Toyota cars are constant at the price of 13k USD)

3.) The line graph shows that from _______ to _________ the _________(subject) ___________(verb in simple past tense).

(ex: From 2011 to 2012 the price of Toyota cars went up. From 2013 to 2014 etc...)

4.) In conclusion, _________.

(ex: In conclusion, Toyota cars are volatile in price )

Pie chart template:

 1.) This pie chart is about ___________.

2.) The Trend of this pie chart shows that __________.

vocab words: popular, amount, highest, lowest, most, least

(ex: Toyota cars are the most popular car and Hyundai is the least popular)
(ex2: Toyota cars have the highest amount of customers and Hundai has the lowest amount of customers)

3.) The pie chart shows that ________________.

vocab words: next, finally, biggest, smallest

(ex: .. Toyota cars are the biggest at 75%, next Honda cars are at 20%, finally Hyundai cars are the smallest at 5%)

4.) In conclusion ____________________.

(ex: In conclusion, Toyota cars are the most popular and Hyundai cars are the least popular.)
